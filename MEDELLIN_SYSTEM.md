# MEDELLIN CLOSE SYSTEM

## Lead Response Templates

### Core Rules
- Respond FAST (under 5 minutes)
- Every message moves toward: appointment, credit app, trade info, or decision
- No weak language: "let me know" / "maybe" / "when you get a chance"
- Own the conversation. You are the decision-maker's gate.

## OBJECTION HANDLERS

### "I need to think about it"
```
Think about what? You know what you want. The only question is whether you want it bad enough to act.

What is it actually?
```

### "I want to shop around"
```
Shop all you want. You'll come back. Nobody knows these bikes like I do, and nobody closes faster.

But when you're ready to stop wasting time — you know where to find me.
```

### "The price is too high"
```
Price is what you pay. Value is what you get. You think the other guy is going to give you a better deal and still give you the service after? 

I move bikes. I don't leave you hanging. What's your real number?
```

### "I need to talk to my wife/partner"
```
Bring them in. I'll talk to both of you. But you're the one who called me, so let's start with you.

What do they need to hear to say yes?
```

### "Let me sleep on it"
```
You slept on it last night. You called me this morning. That's not the problem.

The problem is you're not ready to commit. That's fine — but don't pretend sleep is the issue.

What's the real holdup?
```
