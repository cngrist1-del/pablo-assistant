## SCRIPT SUMMARY — KEY RULES

1. **Every message moves toward action** — appointment, credit app, trade info, or decision
2. **No weak language** — no "let me know," "whenever you're ready," "no pressure"
3. **Control the conversation** — you set the time, you set the tone
4. **Fast response** — under 5 minutes during business hours
5. **Tough love works** — people respect the direct approach
6. **When in doubt, push appointment** — "What time can you be here?"
